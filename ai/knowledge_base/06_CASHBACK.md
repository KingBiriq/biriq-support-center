# 06 — Cashback

## Standard cashback

- Rate: 1%
- Applies to eligible completed services
- Internet Data is excluded
- Added when the order becomes Completed
- Does not expire under the current policy

## Cashback page

The customer can see:

- Buy Item
- Withdrawal

## Buy Item

Cashback can pay all or part of an eligible order. If cashback is insufficient, the remainder may be paid using one supported payment method.

## Withdrawal

- Customer may submit a cashback withdrawal request
- Target processing time: within 24 hours
- Withdrawal is not completed until the system or authorized staff confirms it

## Permanent loss on account deletion

Deleting an account permanently removes:

- Cashback balance
- Giveaway rewards
- Order history
- Login/profile data

Cashback cannot be transferred to a newly created account after deletion.

## AI rules

- Never invent a cashback balance.
- Read the live ledger/balance.
- Do not say a withdrawal was sent unless transaction status confirms it.
- If a completed eligible order did not credit cashback, check cashback ledger and escalate if missing.
