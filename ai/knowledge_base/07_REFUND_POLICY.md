# 07 — Refund Policy

## Instant products

Instant products are not refundable after successful payment and completion.

Examples:

- PUBG Mobile UC
- Free Fire Diamonds
- Internet Data

## Manual products

A manual order may be eligible for refund only when it has not been fulfilled.

Examples:

- PUBG Mobile KR
- eFootball Coins
- Other manual services

Possible refund deduction: 2–5%  
Target refund processing: within 24 hours after approval

The exact deduction and approval must come from authorized policy/configuration or staff decision.

## Completed orders

Completed orders are never refundable.

## Wrong Player ID / account detail

The customer is responsible for the information entered.

- Completed instant delivery to a wrong ID: no refund.
- Unfulfilled manual order: support may review eligibility.
- In some unresolved cases, support may return value as website cashback only when authorized.

## Cancellation

Orders cannot be freely cancelled. A customer may request a refund only when the refund policy allows it.

## AI authority

The AI may:

- Explain policy
- Check eligibility indicators
- Create/escalate a refund review request if tools allow

The AI may not:

- Approve a refund
- Promise a refund
- Change refund amount
- Mark a refund completed without tool confirmation
