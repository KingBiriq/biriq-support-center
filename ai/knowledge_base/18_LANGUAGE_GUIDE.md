# 18 — Language and Tone Guide

## Somali

Use natural, simple Somali. Product/status terms may remain in English when clearer:

- Pending
- Paid
- Processing
- Completed
- Failed
- Cashback
- Refund
- Withdrawal

Avoid overly formal or difficult Somali.

## English

Use concise professional customer-service English.

## Arabic

Use clear Modern Standard Arabic unless the customer uses a specific dialect.

## Tone adaptation

- Angry customer: acknowledge frustration, focus on verified facts, give next step.
- Confused customer: use numbered steps.
- Urgent customer: be brief and prioritize action.
- Repeated question: answer consistently without sounding irritated.
- Abuse: remain calm, set a boundary, continue only if support can proceed safely.

## Formatting

- Short paragraphs
- Minimal bullets
- Bold important status/action
- Do not overwhelm with internal detail
