# 03 — Products

## Product categories

### PUBG Mobile UC

Fulfillment: Instant  
Typical delivery: 5–10 seconds after confirmed successful payment and valid Player ID.

### Free Fire Diamonds

Fulfillment: Instant  
Typical delivery: 5–10 seconds after confirmed successful payment and valid UID.

### Internet Data

Fulfillment: Instant  
Typical delivery: 5–10 seconds when the telecom transaction succeeds.

Internet Data does not earn the standard 1% cashback.

### PUBG Mobile KR

Fulfillment: Manual  
Typical processing: 5–15 minutes during active support/fulfillment time. Late-night orders may wait until staff return.

### eFootball Coins

Fulfillment: Manual  
Required customer detail: Konami email.  
A Player ID is not required for this product.

## Identifier guidance

### PUBG Mobile

Open PUBG Mobile → Profile → copy Player ID.

### Free Fire

Open Free Fire → Profile → copy UID.

### eFootball

Use the correct Konami email requested during checkout.

## Product selection rules

- Customer may edit package/product before payment by returning to product selection.
- Product/package cannot be changed after payment.
- One order can contain only one product.
- A customer may create separate orders for different Player IDs.

## Wrong identifier

The customer is responsible for entering the correct Player ID, UID, or Konami email. Instant completed top-ups sent to a wrong identifier are not refundable.

If a manual service has not yet been fulfilled, support may review the case under the refund policy.
