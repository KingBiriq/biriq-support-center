# 13 — Response Templates

Use templates as guides, not rigid scripts. Replace bracketed fields with verified data.

## Greeting — Somali

Salaan 👋 Ku soo dhowow Biriq Store Support. Sideen maanta kuu caawin karaa?

## Pending payment

Waxaan hubiyey order-kaaga `[ORDER_REFERENCE]`. Status-ku hadda waa **Pending**. Fadlan ha bixin mar kale inta aan payment-kii hore la xaqiijin. `[NEXT_STEP]`

## Paid and processing

Payment-ka order-kaaga `[ORDER_REFERENCE]` waa la xaqiijiyey, order-kuna wuxuu ku jiraa **Processing**. Adeeggan waa manual, waxaana caadi ahaan lagu fulinayaa 5–15 daqiiqo marka kooxda fulfilment-ku shaqaynayso.

## Completed

Order-kaaga `[ORDER_REFERENCE]` wuxuu noqday **Completed**. Adeegga waxaa loo diray xogtii aad ku qortay order-ka.

## Payment deducted, unresolved

Waan fahmay in lacagta lagaa jaray. Waxaan marka hore hubiyey order-ka iyo payment record-ka, laakiin wali xaqiijin buuxda lama helin. Kiiska waxaan u gudbinayaa Human Support anigoo ku lifaaqaya xogta order-ka, si aadan dib ugu celin faahfaahinta.

## Instant refund refusal

Waan ka xumahay dhibaatada. Order-kan wuxuu ahaa adeeg instant ah oo la fuliyey, sidaas darteed policy-ga Biriq Store ma oggola refund kadib completion.

## Manual refund review

Order-kan waa manual, mana muuqdo inuu weli fulfilled yahay. Waxaan u gudbinayaa refund review. Refund-ka lama xaqiijin karo ilaa kooxda awoodda leh ay ansixiso; deduction 2–5% ah ayaa dhici karta.

## Wrong Player ID

Waxaan hubiyey in order-ka lagu fuliyey Player ID/UID-kii lagu qoray checkout-ka. Maadaama adeeggu completed yahay, xogta destination-ka lama beddeli karo refund-na ma jiro.

## Cashback

Order-ka `[ORDER_REFERENCE]` wuxuu u qalmaa 1% cashback. `[CASHBACK_RESULT]`

## Withdrawal

Withdrawal request-kaaga `[REFERENCE]` status-kiisu waa `[STATUS]`. Target processing-ku waa 24 hours, laakiin waxaan sheegi karaa inuu dhammaaday oo keliya marka transaction-ku xaqiijiyo.

## Human handoff

Waxaan kiiskaaga u wareejinayaa qof Support ah. Waxaan la wadaagayaa order context-ka iyo baaritaankii aan sameeyey, si aadan xogta mar kale uga bilaabin.

## Closing

Ma jirtaa arrin kale oo order-kan la xiriirta oo aan kuu hubiyo?
