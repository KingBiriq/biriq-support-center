# 09 — Approved FAQ

## 1. Sideen isku diiwaangeliyaa?
Guji **Continue with Google**, dooro Google Account-kaaga, kadibna dhammaystir xogta lagaa rabo. Guest checkout ma jiro.

## 2. Username iyo password ma isticmaali karaa?
Maya. Login-ku waa Google Account oo keliya.

## 3. Google Account ma khasab baa?
Haa. Waxaa loo baahan yahay diiwaangelin iyo login.

## 4. Google email-ka ma beddeli karaa?
Maya, waqtigan xaadirka ah email-ka ku xiran account-ka lama beddeli karo.

## 5. Haddii aan logout sameeyo sideen kusoo noqdaa?
Guji Continue with Google oo dooro isla Google Account-kii aad markii hore isticmaashay.

## 6. WhatsApp number keliya laba account ma ku samayn karaa?
Maya. Hal WhatsApp number wuxuu ku xirnaan karaa hal account oo keliya.

## 7. Biriq Store shopping cart ma leeyahay?
Maya. Nidaamku waa Single Buy Direct.

## 8. Laba product hal mar ma iibsan karaa?
Maya. Product kasta wuxuu u baahan yahay order gaar ah.

## 9. Laba payment method hal order ma isticmaali karaa?
Laba hab oo dibadda ah lama isku dari karo. Cashback Balance waxaa lagu dari karaa hal payment method.

## 10. Order ma kaydin karaa si aan hadhow u bixiyo?
Maya. Save Order ama Reserve Order ma jiro.

## 11. Maxay yihiin instant products?
PUBG UC, Free Fire Diamonds, iyo Internet Data.

## 12. Instant delivery intee ayay qaadataa?
Caadi ahaan 5–10 seconds kadib payment la xaqiijiyey iyo xog sax ah.

## 13. Maxay yihiin manual products?
PUBG KR, eFootball Coins, iyo adeegyada kale ee gacanta lagu fuliyo.

## 14. Manual order intee ayuu qaataa?
Caadi ahaan 5–15 minutes marka staff-ku shaqaynayo. Habeen dambe waxaa laga yaabaa in subaxda la fuliyo.

## 15. eFootball maxaa la iga rabaa?
Konami email sax ah. Player ID looma baahna.

## 16. PUBG Player ID xaggee ka helaa?
Fur PUBG Mobile, gal Profile, kadibna copy garee Player ID.

## 17. Free Fire UID xaggee ka helaa?
Fur Free Fire, gal Profile, kadibna copy garee UID.

## 18. Wrong Player ID haddii aan geliyo maxaa dhacaya?
Macmiilka ayaa mas'uul ka ah xogta. Instant completed order lama refund-gareeyo.

## 19. Product/package ma beddeli karaa?
Ka hor payment-ka waa la beddeli karaa. Payment kadib lama beddeli karo.

## 20. Order ma cancel-gareyn karaa?
Order si caadi ah looma cancel-gareyn karo. Refund request keliya ayaa la eegi karaa haddii policy-gu oggol yahay.

## 21. Instant order refund ma leeyahay?
Maya, kadib successful completion refund ma jiro.

## 22. Manual order refund ma leeyahay?
Keliya haddii uusan weli fulfilled noqon; 2–5% deduction ayaa dhici karta.

## 23. Refund intee ayuu qaataa?
Target-ku waa 24 hours kadib approval.

## 24. Completed order refund ma leeyahay?
Maya.

## 25. Cashback intee le'eg ayaan helayaa?
1% eligible completed orders.

## 26. Internet Data cashback ma leeyahay?
Maya.

## 27. Cashback goorma ayuu soo dhacaa?
Marka order-ku noqdo Completed.

## 28. Cashback ma dhacayaa?
Maya, current policy-ga kuma dhaco.

## 29. Cashback sideen u isticmaalaa?
Cashback page-ka ka dooro Buy Item.

## 30. Cashback ma withdraw-gareyn karaa?
Haa, Withdrawal request ayaad samayn kartaa; target processing waa 24 hours.

## 31. Cashback iyo EVC ma isku dari karaa?
Haa. Cashback + hal payment method waa la oggol yahay.

## 32. Payment methods kee baa jira?
EVC Plus, Jeeb, Zaad, iyo Sahal.

## 33. Visa ama crypto ma shaqeeyaan hadda?
Maya. Waa qorshe mustaqbal ah ilaa si rasmi ah loo hawlgeliyo.

## 34. Dibadda Soomaaliya ma ka dalban karaa?
Waqtigan hadda international payment ma shaqeeyo. Mustaqbalka Visa/Mastercard iyo Crypto ayaa la qorsheeyey.

## 35. Internet-ku haddii uu go'o inta payment-ka socdo maxaan sameeyaa?
Ha bixin mar kale ilaa AI/Support-ku hubiyo order-ka iyo payment-ka.

## 36. Lacagta waa la iga jaray order-kuna Pending ayuu yahay?
AI-gu marka hore wuxuu hubinayaa payment iyo order records. Haddii aan la xaqiijin karin, Human Support ayaa loo wareejinayaa.

## 37. Order history intee ayuu jiraa?
Wuxuu ku sii xirnaanayaa account-kaagii Google ee asalka ahaa.

## 38. Account-kayga ma tirtiri karaa?
Haa, laakiin account data, history, cashback, iyo rewards si joogto ah ayay u lumi doonaan.

## 39. Order-kayga ma private baa?
Haa. Macaamiisha kale ma arki karaan Player ID, payment, history, WhatsApp, cashback, ama Konami email-kaaga.

## 40. Human Support sideen ula hadlaa?
Ka codso Live Chat-ka in laguu wareejiyo qof Support ah. AI-gu wuxuu gudbinayaa context-ka order-ka iyo baaritaankii hore.
