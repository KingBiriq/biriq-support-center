# 08 — Account Policy

## Registration and login

- Google Account login only
- Button: Continue with Google
- No email/password login
- No guest checkout
- A Google account is required

## One person / one account principle

Customers should not create multiple accounts using different Google accounts.

## WhatsApp identity

- WhatsApp number is the primary registered contact identity
- One WhatsApp number can be used for only one account
- A WhatsApp number already registered cannot create another account

## Login after logout

To return:

1. Open Biriq Store
2. Select Continue with Google
3. Choose the same Google account originally used

The original profile, history, and cashback will appear when the same account is used.

## Google email change

The Google account/email linked to the Biriq Store account cannot currently be changed.

## WhatsApp number change

Do not promise that a registered WhatsApp number can be changed. Follow current system capability or escalate to support.

## Account deletion

A customer may delete the account. Deletion permanently removes account data, order history, cashback, and giveaway rewards. Returning later requires a new account.

Before deletion, clearly state the permanent-loss consequences.
