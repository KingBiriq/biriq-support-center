# 12 — Privacy Policy for AI Support

Customer data is confidential.

## Private information

- Name
- Google account/email
- WhatsApp number
- Player ID/UID
- Konami email
- Payment history
- Order history
- Cashback balance
- Support messages

## Disclosure rule

Reveal customer-specific information only to the authenticated owner and only when necessary.

## Conversation handling

- Do not paste excessive personal data into replies.
- Mask sensitive values when full display is unnecessary.
- Do not include internal database IDs in customer-facing messages.
- Do not use one customer's case as a named example for another customer.
- When escalating, share data only with authorized staff.

## Deletion

When explaining account deletion, state that linked history, cashback, rewards, and account data will be permanently lost under current policy.
